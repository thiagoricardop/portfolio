About me

Who am I

I am a student of Electrical Engineering with a focus on Computing at UFMG and have a passion for technology and innovation. Previously, I had the opportunity to work as a Research Assistant at IT4i, a leading provider of supercomputing services in Europe. During my time there, I collaborated with research and development professionals, maintaining and developing a graphical tool that visualized the output from the MERIC tool using the Python programming language. I also took responsibility for managing and displaying data stored in IT4i's supercomputers, actively contributing to the efficiency and effectiveness of the graphical tool. This experience enhanced my understanding of high-performance computing systems and allowed me to sharpen my skills in data visualization and management. It was a valuable period that further ignited my passion for exploring technological frontiers and driving innovation.


I had also worked as an intern at Ciber Solar, a renowned company in the market for photovoltaic plant projects, where I actively participated in the design of various microgeneration projects, acquiring valuable knowledge and developing important technical skills in the field. I also have experience in web development, having recently participated in the creation of TBB (The Best Bet), a software that collects data and projects gains in online betting houses, enhancing my skills in the software development area.


I am always looking to improve my knowledge and skills, which is why I am taking web development and web design courses to become even more competitive in the market and continue to grow professionally.
